# Modul 8: Data Analysis dan Synthesis

---

## **LANGKAH 1: DESCRIPTIVE ANALYSIS DAN STUDY CHARACTERISTICS**

### **Prompt untuk GenAI:**
```
Berdasarkan complete extracted database dengan [number] studies:

EXTRACTED DATABASE:
[paste summary data dari semua studies: study IDs, designs, populations, interventions, outcomes, key results]

Tolong bantu saya conduct comprehensive descriptive analysis:

1. STUDY CHARACTERISTICS SUMMARY:
   - Publication year distribution dan trends
   - Study design breakdown dengan frequencies
   - Geographic distribution dan settings
   - Sample sizes (range, mean, total participants)
   - Population characteristics aggregate

2. INTERVENTION/EXPOSURE ANALYSIS:
   - Types dan categories of interventions
   - Duration dan intensity patterns
   - Comparison group types
   - Implementation variations

3. OUTCOME MEASUREMENT ANALYSIS:
   - Primary outcomes measured
   - Secondary outcomes patterns
   - Measurement tools diversity
   - Follow-up periods

4. METHODOLOGICAL QUALITY OVERVIEW:
   - Quality score distribution
   - Common strengths across studies
   - Recurring limitations
   - Risk of bias patterns

5. SYNTHESIS PREPARATION:
   - Grouping studies by characteristics
   - Identifying comparable outcomes
   - Assessing data availability for analysis

Berikan comprehensive overview yang ready untuk Results section.
```

**Organize** descriptive results dan prepare untuk deeper analysis.

---

## **LANGKAH 2: THEMATIC ANALYSIS DAN EVIDENCE SYNTHESIS**

### **Comprehensive Synthesis dengan GenAI:**
```
Berdasarkan extracted findings dari [number] studies:

KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY:
[paste key results, conclusions, dan main findings dari setiap study]

RESEARCH QUESTION: [research question]

Tolong conduct systematic thematic analysis:

1. MAJOR THEMES IDENTIFICATION:
   - Primary themes emerging from findings
   - Supporting evidence untuk each theme
   - Frequency of theme occurrence across studies
   - Strength of evidence per theme

2. PATTERN ANALYSIS:
   - Consistent findings across studies
   - Contradictory results dan explanations
   - Dose-response relationships (if applicable)
   - Temporal patterns

3. SUBGROUP ANALYSIS:
   - Findings by study design
   - Results by population characteristics
   - Outcomes by intervention type
   - Geographic atau setting differences

4. EVIDENCE SYNTHESIS:
   - Overall effect direction dan magnitude
   - Confidence in findings
   - Clinical atau practical significance
   - Generalizability assessment

5. GAPS DAN LIMITATIONS:
   - Research gaps identified
   - Methodological limitations impact
   - Missing evidence areas
   - Future research priorities

Berikan comprehensive synthesis yang answer research question.
```

**Develop** evidence synthesis dan key findings.

## **LANGKAH 3: EVIDENCE GRADING DAN FINAL SYNTHESIS**

### **Final Evidence Assessment dengan GenAI:**
```
Berdasarkan complete analysis hasil:

SYNTHESIS RESULTS:
[paste hasil dari thematic analysis dan evidence synthesis]

STUDY QUALITY DATA:
[paste quality assessment summary]

Tolong bantu final evidence evaluation:

1. EVIDENCE GRADING:
   - Overall quality of evidence
   - Confidence in effect estimates
   - Risk of bias impact on conclusions
   - Consistency across studies assessment

2. STRENGTH OF RECOMMENDATIONS:
   - Clinical/practical implications
   - Policy recommendations strength
   - Implementation considerations
   - Target population applicability

3. LIMITATIONS ASSESSMENT:
   - Study-level limitations impact
   - Review-level limitations
   - Publication bias likelihood
   - Generalizability constraints

4. CONCLUSION FORMULATION:
   - Primary research question answer
   - Secondary questions conclusions
   - Practical implications
   - Future research directions

5. MANUSCRIPT PREPARATION:
   - Results section structure
   - Discussion points organization
   - Tables dan figures planning
   - Key messages identification

Berikan comprehensive final synthesis ready untuk manuscript writing.
```

**Finalize** analysis dan prepare untuk writing phase.
